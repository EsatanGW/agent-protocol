# Phase 6: Sign-off

## Goal

Accept the work item-by-item against the acceptance criteria, and confirm that evidence, standards, and residual risks are complete.

## Lean / Full minimums

### Lean minimum
- Confirm that the changed behavior has been verified.
- Record residual risk.

### Full minimum
- Acceptance sign-off.
- Surface-coverage review.
- Evidence summary.
- Residual-risk summary.

## Required sections

- Acceptance criteria.
- Surface coverage.
- Evidence summary.
- Residual risk.
